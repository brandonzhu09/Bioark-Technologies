Custom Cloning Services

We offer various plasmid construction services, complemented by a web design that facilitates customer involvement in the plasmid design process. This comprehensive vector construction service combines technical expertise and innovative technologies to meet the diverse needs of researchers in molecular biology, genetics, and related fields.

Technology Background

  • Molecular Cloning Techniques: The service utilizes advanced molecular cloning methods such as restriction enzyme digestion, ligation, and Gibson assembly to construct vectors efficiently and accurately.
  • Promoter and Selection Marker Selection: Knowledge of various promoters and selection markers ensures that the constructed vectors provide robust expression in the intended host cells.
  • Viral Vector Engineering: For applications requiring stable gene expression, our service includes the design of viral vectors, such as lentiviruses and adeno-associated viruses (AAV), known for their ability to transduce a broad range of cell types.

Service Content

Design and Customization

  • Comprehensive design of expression vectors tailored to specific project needs, including promoter selection, gene insertion sites, and tag additions.
  • Customization options to include various elements such as reporters, selection markers, and regulatory sequences.

Vector Construction

  • Utilization of cutting-edge molecular cloning techniques to construct plasmids or viral vectors.
  • Options for standard plasmid construction, as well as complex designs for lentiviral and AAV vectors.

Validation and Quality Control

  • Rigorous validation of constructed vectors through sequencing to confirm the correct insertion and orientation of genes.
  • Functional assays to assess expression levels and activity of the target genes in appropriate cell models.

Delivery Systems

  • Consultation on the optimal delivery method for the constructed vector based on the target cell type, including standard transfection methods or viral transduction.

Technical Support

  • Ongoing technical support throughout the project, including assistance with transfection protocols and troubleshooting.

Application in Research

Vectors constructed through our service are suitable for various applications, including gene therapy, protein production, and functional studies of gene function and regulation.
